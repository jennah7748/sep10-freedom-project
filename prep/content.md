# Content
Topic: Ultrasound Technology. 

## Overview/Introduction/Context
* Ultrasound Technology is a diagnostic technique used to see images of organs & more inside the body. Tranducers from the ultrasound probes help produce wave frequencies abve 20KHz. Usually, the ultrasound machines are put on the area that needs to be examined. Or sometimes, for closer examination, probes may be placed in the inner of the body. 
* In order to get a degree for Ultrasound Technology, it can vary from 2-4 years depending on the program. You would also need an associate's degree or a bachelor's degree with experience in the science and anatomy. You can see more about this [here](https://nyscas.touro.edu/academics/sonography/) An average salary for a Sonographer is 70k and can range up to 105k.
